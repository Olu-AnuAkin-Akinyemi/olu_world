Bio

øLu AnuAkin is a multidisciplinary artist, griot, and founder from Minnesota, rooted in Yoruba and North American heritage. Working across music, visual art, and cinematic world-building, øLu crafts emotionally layered work that bridges Alternative Soul, Indie R&B, Alternative Folk and conscious Hip-Hop.

His single PWR marked a statement of personal transformation — stepping into power, resilience, and arrival. Preceded by WTFYA, a declaration of confident arrival, the two releases display a glimpse of øLu's range. The forthcoming Afterglow EP expands the sonic palette into cinematic, introspective territory.

As founder of Galorious Expression®, øLu approaches artistry as an integrated practice — sound, image, and story are inseparable. Every project is a world.
